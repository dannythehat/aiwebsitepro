Bella Vista Ristorante

Experience the authentic flavors of Northern Italy in the heart of downtown Portland. Family-owned and operated since 1987, we bring you traditional recipes passed down through four generations.

Our Story

In 1987, Giuseppe and Maria Rossi left their small village in Tuscany with nothing but a dream and their grandmother's recipe book. They opened Bella Vista in a modest 30-seat space, serving authentic Northern Italian cuisine to Portland's growing food scene.

Today, their son Marco continues the family tradition as head chef, having trained at the prestigious Culinary Institute of Florence. Our pasta is made fresh daily, our sauces simmer for hours using San Marzano tomatoes imported directly from Italy, and our wine list features over 150 selections from small Italian vineyards.

Meet Our Team

Marco Rossi

Executive Chef & Owner

Trained at the Culinary Institute of Florence, Marco brings 20 years of experience and a passion for authentic Italian cuisine. He sources ingredients directly from Italy and creates seasonal menus that honor traditional recipes while embracing modern techniques.

Sofia Benedetti

Sous Chef

A graduate of the Rome Culinary Academy, Sofia specializes in handmade pasta and traditional sauces. Her attention to detail and creative flair have earned recognition from Portland's food critics and loyal customers alike.

Antonio Moretti

Wine Director

With over 15 years in the wine industry, Antonio curates our extensive Italian wine collection. He travels to Italy twice yearly to discover small-production wines that perfectly complement our cuisine.
