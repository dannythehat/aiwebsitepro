How can you build a website in under 1 hour? +
Our proprietary AI system automates the entire web development process. After a brief consultation, our AI generates the design, creates all pages, optimizes for SEO, and deploys your website automatically. Human experts review everything to ensure quality.
What's included in each package? +
All packages include AI consultation, responsive design, SEO optimization, contact forms, professional hosting, SSL certificate, and 1-hour delivery. Higher tiers include more pages, advanced features, and premium integrations.
Can I make changes after the website is built? +
Yes! We provide easy-to-use admin access where you can make content changes. For design changes or additional features, we offer affordable update services with the same AI-powered speed.
Do you provide hosting and domain? +
Professional hosting is included in all packages with SSL certificate, daily backups, and 99.9% uptime. Domain registration is available as an add-on service, or you can use your existing domain.
What if I'm not satisfied with the result? +
We offer unlimited revisions during the first 48 hours after delivery. Our AI can quickly implement changes based on your feedback. We're committed to your complete satisfaction.
How does the AI consultation work? +
Our AI conducts an intelligent conversation to understand your business, target audience, design preferences, and specific requirements. This consultation typically takes 10-15 minutes and ensures your website perfectly matches your needs.
Is my website mobile-friendly? +
Absolutely! Every website we create is fully responsive and optimized for all devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile. We test on multiple screen sizes to ensure perfect user experience everywhere.
What about SEO optimization? +
All websites include comprehensive SEO optimization: meta tags, structured data, fast loading speeds, mobile optimization, and search engine friendly URLs. Your website will be ready to rank from day one.
